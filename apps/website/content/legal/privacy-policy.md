# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your username and password. We do not require an email address unless you choose to provide one for password recovery.

### Usage Data

We automatically collect basic request data (page visited, timestamp, IP address) for security monitoring and site improvement. This data is retained for a limited period and automatically pruned.

### Cookies

We use the following cookies:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `sessionid` | Keeps you logged in | Browser session |
| `csrftoken` | Protects against cross-site request forgery | 1 year |
| `cookie_consent` | Remembers your cookie consent choice | 1 year |

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or analytics services.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Authentication** -- to log you in and keep your session secure
- **Security** -- to detect and prevent unauthorized access
- **Improvement** -- to understand how the site is used and make it better

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed using Django's default PBKDF2 algorithm and are never stored in plain text.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. We may disclose information if required by law.

## Your Rights

You may:

- **Access** your personal data through your profile page
- **Update** your information at any time
- **Delete** your account by contacting us

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the site administrator.
